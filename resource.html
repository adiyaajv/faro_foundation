Knowledge Hub/Digital Literacy & Online Safety
Resource 01

Digital Literacy &
Online Safety

Our flagship curricula and toolkits help students, educators, and communities build the skills to navigate the digital world safely, critically, and confidently.

About This Resource Area

Building confident, critical digital citizens

FARO Foundation's digital literacy resources are designed to bridge the gap between access to technology and the ability to use it safely and critically. From secondary school curricula to trainer guides, our materials are grounded in real-world use cases facing Mongolian communities.

All materials are freely available for download and have been co-developed with educators, government partners, and international organizations including Meta and DW Akademie.

Key Objectives

  • 01Equip students with the skills to identify and resist misinformation online
  • 02Train educators to confidently deliver digital safety content in classrooms
  • 03Empower parents and caregivers with practical online safety guidance
  • 04Support policy frameworks for digital literacy in national education systems

Practical toolkits and research supporting organizations in strengthening their cybersecurity posture and embracing responsible digital transformation.

About This Resource Area

Securing organizations for the digital age

As digital threats evolve and organizations accelerate their transformation, FARO Foundation provides the practical guidance to help SMEs, government bodies, and civil society navigate cybersecurity with confidence.

Our toolkits are designed to be accessible to non-technical teams, providing step-by-step frameworks that don't require specialized IT staff to implement effectively.

Key Objectives

  • 01Raise organizational awareness of common cyber threats and preventive measures
  • 02Provide actionable frameworks for digital transformation that prioritize ethics
  • 03Support responsible AI adoption across the public and private sectors
  • 04Build lasting internal capacity rather than reliance on external consultants

Research and field studies addressing healthcare access, digital health infrastructure, and community health capacity across Central Asia.

About This Resource Area

Strengthening health systems through knowledge

FARO Foundation's public health work focuses on bridging the gap between health knowledge and community action. Our research illuminates infrastructure gaps, while our toolkits equip frontline workers and community leaders to respond.

These resources draw on field studies conducted across Mongolia's urban and rural aimags, in partnership with international health organizations and local civil society groups.

Key Objectives

  • 01Document and address digital health infrastructure gaps in rural communities
  • 02Equip community health workers with accessible, evidence-based materials
  • 03Reduce stigma around mental health through public awareness campaigns
  • 04Advocate for inclusive health policy that reaches underserved populations

Policy &

Evidence-based policy briefs and advocacy materials designed to influence national frameworks for online safety, digital rights, and inclusive governance.

About This Resource Area

Shaping the policies that protect communities

FARO Foundation's policy work translates on-the-ground insights into actionable legislative recommendations. We engage with parliament, regulatory bodies, and civil society to push for safer, more inclusive digital environments.

Our briefs are used by legislators, NGOs, and international organizations as reference documents in policy design. They are freely available to support advocacy efforts across the region.

Key Objectives

  • 01Develop evidence-based recommendations for national online safety legislation
  • 02Protect children's rights in digital spaces through targeted policy advocacy
  • 03Combat misinformation through regulatory and educational frameworks
  • 04Strengthen civil society's capacity to engage in legislative processes

Knowledge Hub/ESG & Sustainability
Resource 05

ESG &
Sustainability

Starter packs and reporting guides helping Mongolian corporations integrate environmental, social, and governance principles into their operations.

About This Resource Area

Embedding responsibility into business practice

As ESG reporting becomes a global standard, Mongolian corporations face pressure to align with international frameworks. FARO Foundation bridges the gap with practical, accessible resources that don't assume existing expertise.

Our ESG materials draw on established frameworks — GRI, SASB, and UN SDGs — and adapt them to the realities of the Mongolian business environment.

Key Objectives

  • 01Lower the barrier to entry for ESG reporting among Mongolian SMEs and corporates
  • 02Align local business practices with international sustainability standards
  • 03Document and close digital inclusion gaps across Central Asia
  • 04Promote corporate social responsibility as a driver of long-term growth
