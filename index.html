About Us
Our Core Pillars

Founded in 2018, FARO Foundation is a non-governmental organization dedicated to driving sustainable, sector-wide development. We deliver comprehensive solutions across digital transformation, public health, and ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance).

Our primary focus is bridging the gap between human capacity and the complex demands of an evolving world. Through strategic curriculum development, targeted training, and evidence-based policy advocacy, we equip communities, corporations, and government institutions with the practical tools they need to operate securely, ethically and effectively.

Our Partners & Global Network

At FARO Foundation, we recognize that architecting sustainable, systemic change requires collective intelligence and cross-sector collaboration. We strategically align ourselves with world-class institutions — international development agencies, government bodies, leading technology corporations, and grassroots civil society organizations.

By co-creating programs with our partners, we ensure that our interventions are scalable, culturally relevant, and aligned with the highest global standards. Our network includes the United Nations, UNICEF, the European Union, USAID, Meta, OpenAI, and many more.

Our Mission & Approach

Our core mission is to equip individuals and organizations with the necessary skills and knowledge to navigate complex modern environments effectively and safely. To achieve this, we employ a holistic approach.

We focus our operations on key strategic areas: curriculum development, training sessions, policy advocacy, impactful campaigns, and engaging events. By integrating these methods, we ensure that our interventions produce measurable and lasting results.

Our Expertise

We are committed to inclusive digitalization and societal resilience. We actively operate at the forefront of pivotal domains, including cyber security, online safety, child protection, combating misinformation, and addressing cyberbullying.

Through our diverse cross-sectoral initiatives, we endeavor to equip the public with the tools to engage with digital technologies and emerging societal challenges responsibly and securely.

Our Core Values

Every project, training, and policy initiative we undertake is strictly guided by our institutional values:

  • Collaboration
  • Life-long learning
  • Inclusivity
  • Empowerment
  • Ethical use of technology
  • Critical thinking
  • Community engagement
